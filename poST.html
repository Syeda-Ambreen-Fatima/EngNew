CHILDHOOD MEMORISE


In sunny squares and scraped-up knees
A symphony of childhood ease.
Cardboard castles, dreams take flight
Laughter rang in the fading light.

Crayon worlds on weathered walls
Imaginary jungle calls.
Sticky fingers, pockets full
Treasures gathered, beautiful.

Muddy puddles, splashed with glee
Barefoot steps by the endless sea.
Fireflies blinking in the dusk
Whispered secrets, gentle husk.

The passage of time moves steadily
Childhood echoes, soft and low.
A bittersweet and cherished rhyme
Forever engraved in the sands of time.



WRITTEN BY MAHAD ALI
